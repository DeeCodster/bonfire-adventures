Highlights

The 133rd China Import and Export Fair is here! Popularly known as Canton Fair, this is one of the best trade fairs in the world. Get the chance to win more business opportunities, meet suppliers and hold one on one meetings in your area of interest. The fair has collaborated with nearly 3,500 high-quality enterprises in the clothing field, electronics, textiles and garments, office supplies, recreation products, medicines, medical devices and health products. Bonfire Adventures has successfully secured an excellent package giving you the chance to browse through the state-of-the-art exhibits, glimpse into the unique charm of the various folk customs, including outdoor excursions, dragon boat racing, and benefit with a great business turnover.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • DAY 1 ARRIVAL AT CHINA GUANGZHOU

    Ni Hao! Welcome to Guangzhou, the third largest city in China. Upon arrival at Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport, you will be met by the Bonfire Adventures Guide who will assist you with your luggage and drive you to your hotel. Settle inside the air-conditioned vehicle and enjoy the ride through the beautiful Guangzhou, the third largest city in China. The rest of today is on your own to explore this modern metropolis. You can explore the city or sit back and relax as you enjoy the hotel amenities.

  • DAY 2 REGISTRATION FOR THE CANTON FAIR

    After breakfast, the Bonfire Adventures tour representative will drive to your hotel and pick you up for the registration of the Canton Fair and the venue inspection. The fair has quite a number of volunteers, more than the audience. The scene of the event is in the form of stalls and you are guaranteed to pass quite a number of stalls. On this day, our guide will be there to assist you. His guidance will make the process hassle free and he can even act as an interpreter incase you do not speak the same language as the suppliers. You have time to sample products at your own free will ranging from electronics, lighting equipment, machinery, chemical products, vehicle and motor cycle spares, health products, home decorations, office supplies, medical devices, chemicals, household, furniture among others. After lunch, you are free to spend the rest of the day at leisure as you please.

  • DAY 3 AND DAY 4 CANTON FAIR

    After breakfast today you will be picked up by a shuttle bus and dropped at the Canton Fair pavilion. The in-person fair for Phase II and Phase III remains one of the most important for many businesses and draws quite a large crowd. Get the opportunity to view many showcased products, meet with potential buyers, and network with other industry professionals. For Phase II the scope of exhibits includes, Consumer Goods, Toys, Festive and Gifts, Furniture and Home Decor, Tableware, Ceramics and Gardening. Phase III exhibitors include textiles fabrics apparel, shoes, office luggage & leisure products, pharmaceuticals, healthcare and food. Bonfire Adventures can provide a skilled market guide at an additional cost of USD 160 only. At the end of the day our guide will pick you up from the fair and drive you back to your resort for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 5 TOURING GUANGZHOU

    October to November is one of the best times to visit Guangzhou with very beautiful weather. Our itinerary ensures that you spend one or two extra days exploring the gorgeous Guangzhou. After breakfast our guide will pick you up and take you on a tour to Yue Xiu park; the largest city park in Guangzhou. Stroll leisurely in the verdant park to see the Five Rams Statue from which you will savour the interesting legendary story of the five rams known why Guangzhou is called the City of Rams. Next stop is the Beijing Shopping Road. This is one of the most popular pedestrian street in Guangzhou. Featuring high street brand names, designer copies and other local brands. Here you are guaranteed to pick out excellent clothing pieces, accessories, gadgets at a very affordable price. Later, return to your hotel then relax at leisure and afterwards in the late afternoon you will be picked for the Pearl River Cruise. This cruise is the perfect way to relax and enjoy the peacefulness of the city as the cruise slices through the Pearl River; The third largest river in China and the largest in Guangzhou. Enjoy the rich culture and delicious Cantonese food as you sail by sparkling landmarks such as Canton Tower, Hainxshai Island and Guangzhou Bridge. After the 70-minute cruise our guide will drop you back at your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 6 AND 7 LEISURE DAYS IN GUANGZHOU

    On these days you will be free to explore this modern metropolis on your own terms. Guangzhou is filled with a ton of tours for leisure and fun! If you would like, feel free to get some practical suggestions from our tour guide. You can choose to tour the city and experience the essence of China or simply relax and rejuvenate in your resort enjoying the excellent amenities and services. We believe having these free days during the interval of the Canton Fair is guaranteed to add relaxation and joy to your business trip.

  • DAY 8 AND DAY 9 CANTON FAIR

    After breakfast on these days you will be picked up by a shuttle bus and dropped at the Canton Fair Pavilion where you will continue to win more business chances. Window shop, sample products, purchase everything that you would like at that excellent deal and enjoy. At around 6:00pm in the evening, our guide will be there to pick you and return you to your resort.

  • DAY 10 DEPARTURE FROM GUANGZHOU

    Today you will say goodbye to Guangzhou. After breakfast enjoy all the hotel amenities while completing all the formalities right before you check out. To make your departure stress-free, our guide will pick you from your resort hours before your flight. You will arrive at the airport at a good time allowing you to breeze through the airport on your way home. The flight back to Nairobi will take approximately 10 hours 55 minutes. Transport back to your home or hotel will be on your own arrangement.
