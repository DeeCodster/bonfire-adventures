Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day 1 Arrival Nairobi

    Takes approximately 1 hour's drive depending on traffic Bonfire Adventures guide driver will warmly meet you on arrival at the Jomo Kenya a International Airport, transfer you your hotel. Check-in then we brief you on what will hopefully be your best holiday in Africa. Enjoy overnight.You are booked on Bed & Breakfast.

  • DAY 2 Nairobi-Lake Nakuru National Park

    Approximately 170KMs, takes Approximately 3.5 Hours drive depending on traffic. Enjoy Breakfast then we drive you to Lake Nakuru national Park with a brief stop at Great Rift Valley view-Point. We will be arriving at your Lodge/Camp for check-in then lunch. Enjoy an afternoon game viewing drive. The park is famous for the abundant greater and lesser Flamingoes (when the later levels on the lake are low) Wildlife here is bountiful including Lion, Cheetah, Leopard and Hyenas. Rothschild Giraffe as well as black and white Rhino are also found here are. Lake Nakuru National Park is an Ornithological Paradise for Bird lovers as well. Enjoy dinner and overnight.

  • DAY 3 Lake Nakuru National Park-Maasai Mara National Reserve

    Approximately 270 KMs, takes approximately 6 hours drive. Enjoy breakfast then we pick you and drive you to the Maasai Mara National Reserve. This is arguably one of the best Africans Safaris destinations. You will be arriving at Lodge/Camp for check-in then lunch. Proceed on an afternoon game viewing drive .Later return back to the camp for dinner and overnight.

  • DAY 4 Maasai Mara National Reserve

    Enjoy breakfast then depart with packed lunch for a full day of game viewing drives. You can also opt for a morning and afternoon game viewing drives with hot lunch at the lodge. This famous game reserve is world famous for the spectacular wildbeast migration between July through to September each year, the abundant wildlife including 'Big Five' of Africa. The Maasai Mara National Reserve is home to hundreds of wildlife species including the famous Big Five (Lion, Savannah Elephant, Rhino, Leopard and cape Buffalo),the Cheetah, Hartebeests, grand and Thompson Gazelles, Maasai Giraffe, Hippo and common Zebra among many others. Enjoy dinner and Overnight.

  • DAY 5 Departure

    Approximately 290Kms, takes approximate 7hour's drive depending on traffic. Enjoy breakfast then check-out. We drive you back to Nairobi which takes proximately 7hour's. Alternatively at an extra cost you may opt for a one-hour light scheduled flight to the Wilson Airport. If time allows you will enjoy lunch before our guide driver drops you at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your departure flight. Thank you for allowing Bonfire Adventures to host you on Kenya Safari.
